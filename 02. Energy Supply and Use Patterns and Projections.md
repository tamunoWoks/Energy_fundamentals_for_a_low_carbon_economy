# Introduction to Energy Landscapes: Today and Tomorrow
Energy supply and demand projections are challenging to make due to the numerous unknowns that influence our predictions. One thing is clear, though: we need more energy! Population growth and improving living conditions around the world will only drive the demand for energy higher. Regional supply and use differences mean that optimal solutions to our energy challenges may not always be the same.

There is a range of options to supply our energy needs, and one concept that we should keep in mind is **energy density**. Some energy sources have been in use for many years or decades, but can be improved for better efficiency. Others are still emerging, and it is unclear how they may evolve and to what extent they’ll become a part of our energy mix. As we strive to improve our standard of living, increased energy demand will require us to improve **energy efficiency** and reduce our **energy intensity**.
