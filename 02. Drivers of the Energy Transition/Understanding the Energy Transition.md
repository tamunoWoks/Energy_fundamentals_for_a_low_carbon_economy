# Intorduction to Understanding the Energy Transition
The energy transition is reshaping our world right now, yet many people aren't familiar with what it actually means, despite the fact that it will likely touch every aspect of your life.

We'll dive deeper into the energy transition to understand what it is, how long it might take, and what it could cost. To boost our energy literacy and help others do the same, we need to get comfortable with energy terminology. We'll examine the key terms we'll encounter in energy discussions, clarify their meanings, and identify which ones communicate most clearly to avoid confusion.

The transition will take time, but we don't know exactly how quickly it will unfold.

Scenarios help us articulate what actions might be needed to achieve different timelines. Since scenarios involve "looking into the future," you must carefully analyze the factors that affect whether any particular scenario is realistic. The costs and investment requirements will be substantial, but here's the key point: the longer we wait, the more expensive it becomes.
